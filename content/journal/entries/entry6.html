Glaciers@MIT Internship

Summer 2021

With the Glaciers@MIT group, I created an open source Python package for working with large amounts of raster data on Google Earth Engine. It includes capabilities for sorting, filtering, and segmenting images on the cloud, and then downloading results to disk.

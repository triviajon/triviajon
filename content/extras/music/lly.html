Love Like You

Second time producing an acapella recording!

My opinion is that this turned out really well! I was musical director at the time of recording. This time around, we had learned from our recording of Reflection, so we applied the following changes:

  • Record people individually, using a higher quality setup. We didn't have access to a recording studio, so we used a practice room on campus with an AT2020 + audio interface, with a pop filter and wind screen.
  • Record multiple takes of each person to splice them together for the best take of each person possible.
  • Work with FL Studio instead of Audacity, which gave us access to better plugins and a much easier workflow.

I was primarily responsible for the production, but recieved a good amount of help from Silu Shen. The arrangement itself was a collaboration effort between Kelly Hong and I. The sheet music can be found here.

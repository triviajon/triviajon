Counting Stars

Original Arrangement Performed!

This is entirely my arrangement of "Counting Stars" by OneRepublic. It was performed at the MIT 2023 Family Weekend Acapella Concert (FWACC) for somewhere between 300-500 people. This was the "group song" for FWACC, meaning members from all acapella groups at MIT participated in this performance. Rehearsals were run by me and my ex-co-Musical Director of the Asymptones.

Sheet music is avaliable.
